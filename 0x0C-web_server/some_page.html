Femke Bol is Awesome!

Some of Her Achievements:

  • European Athletics Indoor Championships gold medalist in 400m and 4x400m relay.
  • Set multiple Dutch records in 400m hurdles and other events.
  • Reached the final in the 400m hurdles at the Tokyo 2020 Olympics.
  • Winner of Diamond League events in 400m hurdles.
  • Continuously breaking her own personal records and achieving remarkable times.
